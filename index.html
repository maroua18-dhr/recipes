Classsic Vanilla Cupcakes

Prep time : 15mins | Cook time: 18-22 mins

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup milk

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 175Â°C (350Â°F). Line a muffin tin with paper cupcake liners.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In a large bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.
  5. Add the dry ingredients in three additions, alternating with the milk, beginning and ending with the dry ingredients. Mix until just combined.
  6. Divide the batter evenly among the cupcake liners.
  7. Bake until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

Gratin Au Poulet

Prep time: 15 mins | Cook time: 1 hour

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts (or thighs), cooked and shredded
  • 4 medium potatoes, peeled and sliced thinly
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 1/2 cups grated cheese
  • 1 tbsp olive oil or butter
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 190Â°C (375Â°F).
  2. Heat the olive oil or butter in a pan over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and garlic, and sautÃ© until soft and fragrant, about 3-4 minutes.
  3. In a large bowl, mix the shredded chicken, sautÃ©ed onions and garlic, salt, pepper.
  4. Grease a baking dish with butter or oil. Layer the bottom of the dish with a portion of the sliced potatoes.
  5. Add a layer of the chicken mixture on top of the potatoes.
  6. Repeat the layers until all potatoes and chicken mixture are used, finishing with a layer of potatoes on top.
  7. In a separate bowl, mix the cream and milk, and pour this mixture over the layers in the baking dish.
  8. Sprinkle the grated cheese evenly over the top.
  9. Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake for 40 minutes.
  10. After 40 minutes, remove the foil and bake for another 20 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and bubbly.
